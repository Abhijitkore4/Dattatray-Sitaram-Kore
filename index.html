FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Filing an Income Tax Return helps you stay compliant with tax laws. It is also useful when applying for loans, visas, government tenders, and claiming income tax refunds. Even if your tax liability is low, filing your ITR can provide financial proof for future requirements.

GST Registration is mandatory for businesses crossing the prescribed turnover limit and for certain categories of businesses. It also allows you to issue GST invoices, claim Input Tax Credit (ITC), and conduct business legally across India.

A PAN Card is essential for filing Income Tax Returns, opening bank accounts, investing, purchasing property, applying for loans, and many other financial transactions. It serves as a unique identity for taxation purposes.

Proper accounting helps you monitor your business income and expenses, prepare financial statements, file taxes accurately, and make informed business decisions. It also reduces the chances of penalties during audits.

An FSSAI License is mandatory for most food-related businesses such as restaurants, hotels, bakeries, grocery stores, food manufacturers, and food delivery businesses. It ensures food safety compliance and increases customer trust.
